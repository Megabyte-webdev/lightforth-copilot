**LightForth Copilot** is a tactical command center platform for defense and armored vehicle operations.  
It enables real-time monitoring, coordination, and command over tactical units, responding to emergency signals, and providing critical support to personnel in the field.  
The Copilot also integrates **real-time meeting/session detection** for tactical coordination via Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet.

## 🛡 Key Features

- **Vehicle & Unit Tracking** – Monitor armored and tactical units in real-time.
- **Emergency Alerts** – Receive, prioritize, and respond to alerts instantly.
- **Operational Dashboard** – Visualize troop movements and command assignments.
- **Command & Control** – Issue orders to units on the ground efficiently.
- **Tactical Meeting Detection** – Detect live coordination sessions on Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet.
- **Cross-Platform Support** – Runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Secure Communication** – End-to-end encrypted messaging between command center and field units.

## 🛠 Installation

### macOS

1. Download the `.app.tar.gz` for your architecture.
2. Extract and move **lightforth.app** to the Applications folder.
3. If blocked on launch:
   - Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security**
   - Click **Open Anyway**

### Windows

1. Download the `.exe` or `.msi` installer.
2. Run the installer and follow the setup wizard.
3. Launch LightForth from the Start Menu.
