**Lightforth Copilot** is an intelligent background utility designed for seamless tactical coordination and session management. It acts as a silent partner on your workstation, monitoring environment signals and providing real-time AI assistance during critical operations.

By integrating **real-time meeting and audio session detection**, Lightforth ensures that command personnel remain focused on the field while the Copilot handles documentation, transcriptions, and session logistics across Zoom, Teams, and Google Meet.

### What’s New

- **Automated Meeting Detection** – Intelligent detection of active sessions on Zoom, Microsoft Teams, and Google Meet.

- **Advanced Audio Capture** – Real-time processing of system and microphone audio for live coordination.

---

## Key Features

- **Intelligent Session Latching** – Automatically detects when a tactical meeting starts and launches the appropriate support widgets.
- **System Tray Architecture** – Runs silently in the background with a "Hide-to-Tray" workflow to keep your workspace clear.
- **Stealth Mode** – Operates without appearing in the taskbar or dock during active monitoring to minimize distractions.
- **AI-Powered Insights** – Real-time query capabilities to pull tactical data during live sessions.
- **Cross-Platform Parity** – Consistent performance and feature set across Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Privacy-First Design** – Localized processing of session signals with encrypted transmission to command nodes.

## Installation

### macOS

1.  Download the `.app.tar.gz` for your architecture (Intel or Apple Silicon).
2.  Extract and move **Lightforth.app** to your `/Applications` folder.
3.  **Security Note:** If blocked on first launch, navigate to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **"Open Anyway"**.

### Windows

1.  Download the `.exe` or `.msi` installer.
2.  Run the installer and follow the setup wizard.
3.  Launch **Lightforth** from the Start Menu. It will automatically initialize in your System Tray.
